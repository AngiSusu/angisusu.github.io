I'm

hi, i'm angelina. i'm an atl-born passionate programmer more or less attached at the hip to my keyboard. i also love art, music, travel, physics, and dance. if you can't find me, there's a good chance i'm on an airplane, but my laptop is always close at hand!

i started out as an artist, then a writer, then a gamer, and a programmer. all of this was before middle school. these days, i am a programmer, but also a wandering nomad, travel enthusiast, avid reader, and pianist. i am outgoing, enjoy sharing meals with friends, and staying out late. i am passionate about good dessert, aesthetic bakeries, the oxford comma, and garlic powder. it is very nice to meet you :)
